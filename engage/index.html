JOIN US

SUBSCRIBE
H-Net subscribers can make tangible contributions to their field by joining and contributing to H-Net networks - communities of scholars with shared scholarly interests. Networks are moderated by editorial teams and advisory boards composed of trained scholars and field experts.

PROPOSE A NETWORK
H-Net networks are powerful tools to shape scholarly discourse and pedagogical practices. Because of their underlying platform and overarching governing structure, they wed digital communications with academic best practices in ways that can be particularly appealing within some fields of study.

BECOME AN EDITOR
Network Editors are responsible for managing and maintaining the vitality and professional quality of their networks. The work Network Editors do is diverse, and includes everything from creating and managing teaching and research resources, to producing podcasts and videos. Review Editors choose books for review and seek out qualified reviewers for them. Reviews are published on the H-Net Commons (via individual networks).

BECOME AN ADVISORY BOARD MEMBER
Advisory Board Members work to support their editorial teams, set network-level policies, and adjudicate any disputes that arise within or against editorial teams.
